Frequently Asked Questions

What is this app used for?
This app helps you organize notes, ideas, and personal information in a simple and efficient way. It is designed to keep your thoughts structured and accessible at all times.
How do I create a note?
Click on “Add Note”, write your content, and save it to your cloud storage. You can also add images and tags to make your notes more descriptive and easier to find later.
Can I upload images?
Yes, you can upload images directly from your device and attach them to notes. Just click the "Upload Image from Device" button in the Digital Cloud section before saving.
Is my data private?
Yes, your personal cloud data is stored privately and is only visible to you. We use local browser storage to ensure that your information stays on your own device.
Can I access my notes anywhere?
Yes, your notes are synced and available from any device as long as you use the same browser and environment. We ensure a smooth experience across different screen sizes.
