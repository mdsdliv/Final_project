FAQ

How to save music?
Just upload any MP3 file in the top right corner. It stays as long as the page is open!
Where are my notes stored?
Everything is saved in your browser's LocalStorage. Private and safe.
Can I delete a note?
Yes, each note card in the Search section has a delete button.
